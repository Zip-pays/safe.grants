FAQ

Questions, answered.

Is this really non-repayable?

Yes. Grants are gifts. Recipients never repay the amount awarded and are not required to pay interest, dividends, or fees on the award.

How long does it take?

Most decisions are issued within 72 hours of a completed application. Disbursement follows within 24–48 hours of approval.

Which countries are eligible?

The 2026 cycle accepts applicants in North America, Europe, the UK, Australia, and select countries in Africa and the Caribbean.

How do I confirm I'm speaking with an official officer?
